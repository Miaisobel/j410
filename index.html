That's The Key A Cappella

That's The Key A Cappella is an all-vocal music group. The group consists of students from Cal Poly San Luis Obispo who sing covers of songs without instruments. Found in 2009, the group has a sweet style, kicking repertoire, and chaotic good energy.

Most of the pictures and people you will see here are from the 2022-2023 school year. After a quarantine-filled spring quarter the year prior, the group is back in action and ready for anything!
